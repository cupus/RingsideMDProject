The waiting game.

Healthcare in Canada is universally covered but is not universally accessible. 57% of Ontarians wait 2 or more days to see a family doctor*. Each appointment takes on average 2 - 3 hours, much of which is spent in travel and waiting rooms. Patients often require multiple visits to address a single health concern.

The Canadian Fraser Institute reports Canadians wait an average of 10.6 weeks from seeing a specialist to treatment. That doesn’t include the wait times to see a family doctor and the time it takes to be referred to a specialist.

Ringside MD removes barriers to accessing a family doctor. We offer a single point of contact for healthcare services.

Source – *2016 Annual Report of the Office of the Auditor General of Ontario

Annual group insurance premium increases are driven by prescription drugs.

Health and Wellness products have limited success in controlling these costs.

The Xerox 2016 Canadian Health Care Trend Survey Results showed increases in group insurance health claim costs to be greater than 11% annually. This trend is expected to continue and the impact is compounded year-after-year. A rise in claims means increases in premiums for the employer and employee.

Even the best wellness products are limited in their scope and usefulness. They do not integrate well with our healthcare system. Ultimately, your employees are referred back to their Family Doctor off site.
